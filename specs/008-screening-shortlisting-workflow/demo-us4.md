# Demo US4: Detect and Resolve Duplicate Candidates

1. Ensure US3 has been completed.
2. Login as an HR Admin.
3. Navigate to HR Dashboard -> Requisitions.
4. Open the requisition.
5. Click "Check Duplicates" in the management actions.
6. Verify duplicate suggestions appear with confidence categories.
7. Click "Review" on a pair.
8. Select "Ignore (Not duplicates)", provide a reason, and click "Save Decision".
9. Verify the pair no longer appears in the suggestions.
10. Click "Check Duplicates" again, select another pair (if available), choose "Merge Profiles", select a primary, provide a reason, and click "Save Decision".
