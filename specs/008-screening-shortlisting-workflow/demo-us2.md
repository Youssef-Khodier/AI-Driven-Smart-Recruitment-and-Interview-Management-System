# Demo US2: Recalculate Match Scores and Shortlist

1. Ensure US1 has been completed and screening rules are configured for a requisition.
2. Login as an HR Admin.
3. Navigate to HR Dashboard -> Requisitions.
4. Open the configured requisition.
5. Click "View Shortlist".
6. Click "Recalculate Scores".
7. Verify success message appears and applications are listed with updated scores.
8. Verify candidate ordering (Match Score DESC, Years Experience DESC).
9. Check for "Missing: {Skill}" tags on candidates with missing evidence.
